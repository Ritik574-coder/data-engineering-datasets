# 🛍️ Retail Analytics Dataset

## 📖 Overview

The **Retail Analytics Dataset** is a synthetic, enterprise-style retail dataset designed for learning and practicing **Data Engineering**, **Analytics Engineering**, **Data Warehousing**, **SQL**, **Python**, **dbt**, **Apache Spark**, and **Business Intelligence**.

The dataset simulates the daily operations of a retail business by covering the complete sales lifecycle, including customers, products, stores, employees, inventory, sales transactions, product returns, and customer reviews.

It is specifically designed for building end-to-end data engineering projects, ETL/ELT pipelines, dimensional models, and analytical dashboards.

---

# 📌 Business Context

This dataset represents a retail company operating multiple stores across different locations.

Customers purchase products from stores, employees process transactions, inventory levels are monitored through periodic snapshots, customers can submit reviews after purchases, and returned products are tracked for operational analysis.

The dataset closely resembles the structure of real-world retail operational systems and is suitable for practicing enterprise data engineering workflows.

# 🔗 Data Relationships

![Data Lenage Digram][file_image.image]

The dataset follows a relational model where:

- Customers place sales transactions.
- Products are sold through sales transactions.
- Stores process customer sales.
- Employees handle transactions.
- Products belong to inventory snapshots.
- Stores maintain inventory.
- Customers submit reviews for purchased products.
- Customers may return purchased products.
- Employees manage return processing.
- Employees belong to stores and support manager hierarchies.
